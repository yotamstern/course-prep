FHS- 
.git/HEAD - points to what commit the HEAD is at
.git/refs - points to human readable aliases of commit hashes.
.git/objects - points to commits in hex format that leads to their commit hash.
.git/logs - is the log dir for the different branches and their commits. 1 main file for the HEAD + a sub directory for the sub branches.
